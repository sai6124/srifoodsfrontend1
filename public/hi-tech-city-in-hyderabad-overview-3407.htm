About Hi Tech City, Hyderabad

  • Overview

Hitech City, Hyderabad is a hub for technology and engineering. The city is home to several multinational corporations such as Microsoft, Amazon, and Infosys. This modern city is also home to several startups. It is also one of the hottest locations for home buyers. There are plenty of entertainment options and world-class institutions. Cyber Towers is another part of HITEC City. A large amount of space has been made available for various IT companies such as Oracle Corporation, Microsoft, and GE Capital. It features a glass facade and landscaped gardens. One can find five lakh square feet of executive space in Cyber Towers.

Brief Description

Hitech city, a part of Hyderabad, is a thriving IT hub in the state. Originally commissioned by the erstwhile combined Government of Andhra Pradesh, the city was planned as a knowledge enclave. Many renowned technology firms have set up operations in the area. Moreover, non-IT companies are also expanding. These factors have contributed to the increasing demand for residential property in the area.

The upcoming development in Hitech city has a lot of promise. Located close to the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, the city is expected to experience an increase in its infrastructure. In addition, the social life in the city has increased as well. Purchasing a property in this area will also give you a high return on investment, as it has a stable foundation for future growth.

The city is also home to various shopping malls. One can visit the Forum Sujana Mall in the CBD. The mall has various shops and restaurants. In addition, the Shilpakala Vedika convention centre is a great place to watch comedy and music. Other than these, the city is also a popular destination for students. Many colleges and universities are located within the vicinity. Furthermore, the Hyderabad International Convention Centre is also a short distance away.
